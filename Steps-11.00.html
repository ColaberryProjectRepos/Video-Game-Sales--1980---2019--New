Creating “Calendar Table” 11

1.      Go to Modeling Tab and click on New Table button.
2.      Type or Copy & Paste the DAX formula below in the DAX formula bar, Click on the check mark, and Save.


DAX Formula : 


Calendar = CALENDAR(DATE(1980,1,1), DATE(2019,12,31))
