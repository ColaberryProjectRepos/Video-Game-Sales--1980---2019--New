Creating Refresh Date Table 10

1.      Click on Get Data and select Blank query from the list and click on Connect
2.      On the DAX formula bar type ' =DateTime.LocalNow()'
3.      Under the query change the name to 'Refresh Date'
4.      Click on Convert to Table button
5.      Rename the column by right clicking and write ‘DateTime’
6.      Click on Close & apply button and save.
