Problem Statements 1

The video game industry needs help with sales declining. Sales increased to $5 billion from 2000-2009, but declined to $2 billion in 2010. In 2017, the industry lost $60 million in sales compared to the  previous year. Sales decline varies by platform and genre, with some companies seeing growth while others decline. Analyzing sales data and creating a targeted marketing strategy is crucial to revive the industry.
