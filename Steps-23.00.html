CREATING Platform Groups 23


1-     Right click on Platform and select “New Group”
2-     Select “X360, XB, and XOne” while holding down the Left-CTRL key then click on           “Group”.
3-     Rename Group to Xbox
4-     Select “PS, PS2, PS3, PS4, PSP, and PSV” while holding down the Left-CTRL key             then click on “Group”.
5-     Rename the Group to PlayStation
6-     Select “3DS, DS, GB, GBA, GC, N64, NES, SNES, Wii, and WiiU” while holding              down the Left-CTRL key then click on “Group”.
7-     Rename the Group to Nintendo
8-     Select All what’s left and click on “Group”.
9-     Rename the Group to Others.
10-   Click OK to save and close Groups window.
