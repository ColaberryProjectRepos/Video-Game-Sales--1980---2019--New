Adding Canvas Background 15

  1. Download the 'VGS - Logos and Wallpaper.ZIP'  file by clicking the red icon above.
  2. Click anywhere on canvas, go to Format report page in visualizations, and click on Canvas background.
  3. In the Image click Browse and select VGS-Wallpaper.jpg and click open.
  4. Set the Image Fit = Normal, Transparency = 94%
